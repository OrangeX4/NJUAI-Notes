# Problem 6

$定义运算*, 运算表如下:$

| $*$ | $a$ | $b$ |
| --- | --- | --- |
| $a$ | $b$ | $a$ |
| $b$ | $b$ | $a$ |

$\because a*b=a, b*a=b, a*b\neq b*a$

$\quad (a*b)*b=a, a*(b*b)=b, (a*b)*b=a*(b*b)$

$\therefore 该运算在A上不可交换也不可结合.$
